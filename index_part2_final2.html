1. Competitor Weakness Finder

Extract structural failure patterns from competitor reviews and weaponize them into positioning angles.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS POSITIONING COPY
PURPOSE
Turn competitor reviews into a positioning blueprint. Most sellers read reviews for sentiment. You'll extract structural weaknesses and convert them into differentiation angles.
VALUE
Goal: Build a positioning strategy rooted in provable customer pain, not guesswork.
CONTEXT
You'll paste competitor reviews into ChatGPT. The AI will analyze patterns, categorize complaints, and output actionable positioning angles you can use in your copy, product design, or ad hooks.
PROMPT 
You are a competitive intelligence analyst for e-commerce brands.

I will provide you with customer reviews from a competitor's product listing.

Your task:
1. Identify the top 5 recurring complaints or pain points mentioned across reviews.
2. Categorize each complaint by type (e.g., product quality, shipping, customer service, misleading claims).
3. For each complaint, provide:
   - The exact customer language used (quote 2-3 examples)
   - The underlying emotional driver (e.g., frustration, distrust, disappointment)
   - A positioning angle I can use to differentiate my product

Output format:
- Complaint #1: [Category]
  - Customer quotes: "..." / "..." / "..."
  - Emotional driver: [X]
  - Positioning angle: [How to weaponize this in your messaging]

Repeat for all 5 complaints.

6. Trend Risk Filter

Analyze a trending product to determine if it's a 90-day flash or a 12-month opportunity.

PRODUCT VALIDATION TREND ANALYSIS RISK ASSESSMENT
PURPOSE
Avoid trend traps. Distinguish between sustainable demand and hype-driven spikes.
VALUE
Goal: Invest in products with longevity, not fads that die in 60 days.
CONTEXT
You'll describe a trending product. ChatGPT will analyze its sustainability using trend indicators, search volume patterns, and market saturation signals.
PROMPT 
You are a product trend analyst for e-commerce brands.

I will describe a product that is currently trending.

Your task:
1. Assess the product's longevity using these criteria:
   - Search volume trajectory (is it spiking or steady?)
   - Market saturation (how many competitors?)
   - Seasonality risk (is it tied to a specific event/holiday?)
   - Repeat purchase potential (one-time buy or recurring need?)
2. Assign a trend risk score (1-10, where 1 = flash fad, 10 = sustainable demand).
3. Provide a go/no-go recommendation with reasoning.
4. If "go," suggest 2 strategies to extend the product's lifecycle.

Output format:
- Trend analysis:
  - Search volume: [Pattern]
  - Market saturation: [Level]
  - Seasonality: [Risk level]
  - Repeat purchase: [Likelihood]
- Trend risk score: [X/10]
- Recommendation: [Go / No-go]
- Reasoning: [Why?]
- Lifecycle extension strategies (if "go"):
